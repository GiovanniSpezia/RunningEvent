Discover the Milan Run

Get ready to lace up and hit the streets of Milan for a dynamic day of sport, music, and community! Whether you're chasing a personal best or just soaking in the vibes, this event welcomes runners of all levels.

  • 🏃‍♂️ Choose between 10K or 5K scenic routes
  • 🎶 Live music and entertainment zones
  • 🏅 Finisher medals for every participant
  • 💧 Hydration & energy stations along the way
